AFA Women’s Health

Independent contractor supporting digital experience, branding, and podcast production for a women’s health practice — from shoot-day logistics to episode frameworks that keep clinical conversations engaging and on-brand.

UX Strategy & Digital Marketing Consultant · Contractor under practice leadership · Metrics pending public release

Context

AFA needed tighter digital storytelling: cohesive branding for providers, patient-facing content, and a podcast concept (“The Late Period”) that feels natural on camera while staying clinically responsible.

What I did

  • Coordinated branding photoshoot and podcast production logistics — timed provider blocks, room flips, MUA/photographer/producer handoffs.
  • Built podcast segment frameworks (interactive prompts, “Viral or Valid?” voting, guest notes) so recording days stay structured without sounding scripted.
  • Supported website UX / SEO direction and content structure for clearer patient navigation.
  • Kept stakeholder communication clear across clinical and creative teams.

AI used

Outlines and content drafts with ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini — then human-edited for tone, clinical sensitivity, and brand fit.

Why this travels

Client work under constraints: scheduling, brand consistency, content systems, and calm communication with busy stakeholders. Useful in marketing, ops, healthcare admin, communications, and any role that ships with real people on a timeline.

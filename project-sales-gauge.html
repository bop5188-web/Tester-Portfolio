Sales Gauge

Product design · Full site build · Live client

I redesigned and implemented the full Sales Gauge site end to end, set up Google Analytics myself, and turned an Excel High Value Trade workflow into a web product sales managers can share with their teams.

Product Design & Digital Experience Associate · Dec 2024–present · Works with founder · sales-gauge.com

  • +162% users (~1 year, GA I set up)
  • +156% engagement
  • All pages layout → live implementation

Problem

The site looked dated and was hard to use: broken links, unclear navigation, and no clear path to pricing, training, or how to actually get access (it required contacting the company, but that wasn’t obvious). Separately, High Value Trade Tables lived in Excel — hard to share, easy to break, and not something you could sell as a product.

What I owned

  • Redesigned every page — layout through live implementation (not mockups-only).
  • Fixed navigation, broken links, pricing clarity, and the “contact us to get access” path so people could find what they needed.
  • Stood up social presence to drive attention to the product.
  • Set up Google Analytics and pulled the growth numbers myself (~one-year window: +162% users, +156% engagement).
  • Proposed coding the Trade Table as a web app (founder already used Excel; I pushed it into a more professional, sellable tool). Built the UI and share links; explored auth / data locally and advised the founder on hosting on his own server — production hosting wasn’t my final handoff.

Trade Table — who it’s for

Sales managers create a tracker table in a manager account, lock the setup they want, then share it with their team so reps track deals the same way — multiple tables, logins, and share links instead of a fragile spreadsheet.

Result

Clearer paths to product, pricing, and access; a modern site that matches how the company sells; measurable traffic/engagement growth I can walk through in Analytics; and a Trade Table concept that moved from “internal Excel” to “product we can sell.”

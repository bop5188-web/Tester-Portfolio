Sales Gauge

Redesigned and rebuilt the company’s digital presence with the founder — helping drive +162% monthly users and +156% engagement — then turned an Excel negotiation workflow into a web product concept companies can host and sell.

Dec 2024 – present · Product Design & Digital Experience Associate · Worked directly with the founder · sales-gauge.com

Context

Sales Gauge trains B2B sales teams with live programs, eLearning, and AI coaching tools. The site needed clearer structure, stronger product storytelling, and room to grow beyond a brochure into tools the business can productize.

What I did

  • Led an end-to-end website redesign and rebuild (Squarespace + front-end customization) over a focused launch cycle with the founder.
  • Rebuilt information architecture, navigation, and content hierarchy across training, eLearning, and AI product surfaces.
  • Supported pages that showcase modules and the $pAIce AI Coaching Engine alongside High Value Trades training.
  • Used AI tools (ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini) for social research and lighter content drafting to move faster without losing brand voice.

Product beat: High Value Trade Table

Negotiation teams were stuck in spreadsheet workflows. I proposed moving High Value Trade Tables from Excel into a hosted web app the company could offer as a product — then mocked and built a working prototype with auth, multi-table management, shareable links, MongoDB persistence, and Netlify functions.

  • Idea → mockup (free tools) → working web app architecture
  • Sign up / sign in, create tables, share view-only links
  • Code: github.com/bop5188-web/SalesGaugeTradeTable

AI used

Day-to-day research and social drafts with ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini; site surfaces for Sales Gauge’s own AI coaching product ($pAIce); product thinking for AI-assisted sales tools.

Why this travels

Clear problem → ship with stakeholders → measure with analytics → turn an internal pain into a product. That pattern fits growth, ops, marketing, product, and analyst-adjacent roles — not only design titles.
